# 22 — Analytics and Marketing Readiness

## Goal

Check whether SEO performance can be measured and whether important conversion events are tracked.

## Analytics checks

- Google Analytics or alternative analytics;
- Google Tag Manager;
- Yandex Metrica for RU/CIS projects;
- privacy/consent setup where applicable;
- duplicate tags;
- broken events;
- cross-domain tracking if needed;
- ecommerce events if applicable.

## Conversion tracking

Check whether key actions are tracked:

- form submit;
- click-to-call;
- click-to-email;
- messenger click;
- booking/request flow;
- checkout steps;
- purchase/subscription;
- lead quality where available.

## Marketing pixels

- Meta/Facebook Pixel;
- TikTok Pixel;
- LinkedIn Insight Tag;
- VK/Yandex/other ad pixels.

Only recommend pixels when the business uses or plans paid advertising/retargeting. Do not classify missing pixels as core SEO errors.

## Reporting

Separate:

- SEO measurement;
- conversion analytics;
- paid marketing readiness;
- privacy/compliance constraints.
